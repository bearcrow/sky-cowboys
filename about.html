Sky Cowboys: Greenhorn Trail   is the story of Molly Shepherd ‐ a bookish researcher who's found hard evidence of an elusive creature, thought by many to be a cryptid myth. But she has to learn the ropes of flying a cranky pegasus alongside a rough-and-tumble posse if she's got a chance of crossing the Wild Winds and finding the creature.

Greenhorn Trail is a homage to the classic westerns, and is suitable for a general audience. It is updated mid-month.

No AI was used in the making of Sky Cowboys.




About the Author
Hi, I'm Lauren, also known as “BarnLarn” online. I'm a concept artist and illustrator from the midwest. I've worked in animation, the equestrian industry (everything from summer camps to dressage show barns). Now I design and make costumes and props in Minnesota. Sky Cowboys is my personal project.

My love of horsemanship and the outdoors started at an early age ‐ taking lessons as a little kid, and buying my first horse at 13 for a couple hundred bucks. I met a fantastic trainer shortly after. His methods are a perfect balance of old-school ranch skills, ethical handling, and a high degree of technical finesse. It has shaped not only how I work with horses, but my personal character and worldview. I've soaked up all of his horsemanship knowledge over the years and have ridden, hiked, camped, and climbed all over the hills and mountains of the west coast, and through the forests and lakeshores of Minnesota & Wisconsin.

I started developing Sky Cowboys back when I lived in Los Angeles ‐ inspired by my rides though the wild-west landscape and dramatic vistas of Yosemite, while pining for untouched wilderness as I lived in a crowded city. I created and self-published an artbook full of vibrant concept art, and now Greenhorn Trail is a brand new comic taking place in the world of Sky Cowboys.
